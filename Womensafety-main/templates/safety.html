Safety Tips

Share Your Location

Always share your location with trusted friends or family when traveling alone or meeting someone new.

Emergency Contacts

Keep emergency contact numbers saved in your phone and set up quick dial shortcuts for easy access.

Self-Defense

Consider taking self-defense classes and carrying personal safety devices like pepper spray or a whistle.

Plan Your Route

Plan your route in advance and stick to well-lit, populated areas when walking alone.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, trust your instincts and remove yourself from the situation immediately.

Stay Connected

Keep your phone charged and have backup power sources available for emergencies.

Emergency Procedures

If Threatened

  1. Stay calm and assess the situation
  2. Make noise to attract attention
  3. Use your personal safety device
  4. Run to a safe location
  5. Call emergency services immediately

After an Incident

  1. Find a safe place
  2. Contact emergency services
  3. Inform trusted contacts
  4. Document the incident
  5. Seek professional support
